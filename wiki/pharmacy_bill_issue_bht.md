# pharmacy_bill_issue_bht
Source: `/inward/pharmacy_bill_issue_bht.xhtml`
## Configuration Options
- `Display Total Stock when listing medicines for Inpatients` - controls `display total stock when listing medicines for inpatients` behaviour.
- `Enable Drawer Manegment` - controls `enable drawer manegment` behaviour.
- `Enable the Special Privilege of Canceling CC Bills` - controls `enable the special privilege of canceling cc bills` behaviour.
- `Enable the Special Privilege of Canceling OPD Bills` - controls `enable the special privilege of canceling opd bills` behaviour.
- `Immediate Refund Request for OPO Bills of Any Status` - controls `immediate refund request for opo bills of any status` behaviour.
- `Manage Costing` - controls `manage costing` behaviour.
- `Medicine Identification Codes Used` - controls `medicine identification codes used` behaviour.
- `Nursing IP Billing - Show Rate and Value` - controls `nursing ip billing - show rate and value` behaviour.
- `Pharmacy Bill Support for Native Printers` - controls `pharmacy bill support for native printers` behaviour.
- `Pharmacy Inward Direct Issue Bill is FiveFiveCustom3` - controls `pharmacy inward direct issue bill is fivefivecustom3` behaviour.
- `Pharmacy Inward Direct Issue Bill is PosHeaderPaper` - controls `pharmacy inward direct issue bill is posheaderpaper` behaviour.
- `Set the Original Bill PaymentMethod to Cancelation Bill` - controls `set the original bill paymentmethod to cancelation bill` behaviour.
- `Set the Original Bill PaymentMethod to Refunded Bill` - controls `set the original bill paymentmethod to refunded bill` behaviour.
## Privileges
- `NursingIPBillingViewRates` - requires `NursingIPBillingViewRates` permission.
