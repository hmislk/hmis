# Final Presentations & Career Development

## Session Overview
**Duration**: 2 hours
**Prerequisites**: Completed capstone project
**Session Type**: Presentations and Career Planning

## Learning Objectives
- Present professional-quality business analysis work to stakeholders
- Receive constructive feedback on technical and presentation skills
- Plan career advancement strategies in healthcare business analysis and digital health
- Build professional network and establish ongoing learning plans

## Session Structure

### 1. Capstone Project Presentations (90 minutes)

#### Presentation Guidelines
**Time Allocation per Participant**
- **Presentation**: 8-10 minutes for solution overview and key findings
- **Q&A Session**: 3-5 minutes for questions and discussion
- **Feedback**: 2-3 minutes for constructive feedback and suggestions

**Presentation Structure**
1. **Problem Statement** (1-2 minutes): Clear articulation of healthcare challenge
2. **Analysis Approach** (2-3 minutes): Methodology and stakeholder engagement
3. **Key Findings** (2-3 minutes): Critical insights and recommendations
4. **Solution Design** (2-3 minutes): Proposed improvements and implementation strategy
5. **Business Impact** (1-2 minutes): Expected outcomes and value proposition

#### Evaluation Criteria

**Technical Competency (40%)**
- **Requirements Analysis**: Quality of requirement gathering and documentation
- **Solution Design**: Feasibility and innovation of proposed solutions
- **Healthcare Knowledge**: Understanding of clinical workflows and regulations
- **Technology Integration**: Appropriate use of current and emerging technologies

**Business Analysis Skills (30%)**
- **Stakeholder Management**: Identification and engagement of key stakeholders
- **Business Case Development**: Compelling financial and strategic justification
- **Risk Assessment**: Comprehensive risk identification and mitigation planning
- **Implementation Planning**: Realistic and detailed implementation strategy

**Communication and Presentation (30%)**
- **Clarity and Organization**: Logical flow and clear message delivery
- **Visual Design**: Effective use of slides, diagrams, and supporting materials
- **Stakeholder Adaptation**: Appropriate messaging for target audience
- **Professional Delivery**: Confidence, timing, and response to questions

### 2. Peer Feedback and Learning Exchange (20 minutes)

#### Structured Feedback Process
**Peer Review Format**
- **Strengths Recognition**: Highlight effective approaches and innovative solutions
- **Improvement Suggestions**: Constructive recommendations for enhancement
- **Learning Exchange**: Share insights and approaches across projects
- **Best Practice Identification**: Document emerging patterns and successful strategies

**Group Discussion Topics**
- Most innovative use of 2026 digital health trends
- Most compelling business case and ROI presentation
- Best stakeholder engagement and change management strategy
- Most comprehensive risk assessment and mitigation planning
- Most effective presentation style and communication approach

### 3. Career Development Planning (30 minutes)

#### Healthcare Business Analysis Career Pathways

**Specialist Roles**
- **Clinical Analyst**: Focus on clinical workflows and EHR optimization
- **Population Health Analyst**: Chronic disease management and preventive care
- **Revenue Cycle Analyst**: Billing, coding, and financial process optimization
- **Quality Analyst**: Patient safety, regulatory compliance, and performance improvement
- **Informatics Specialist**: Health information management and data analytics

**Leadership Roles**
- **Senior Business Analyst**: Leading complex projects and mentoring junior analysts
- **Business Analysis Manager**: Managing BA teams and organizational capability
- **Clinical Informatics Director**: Strategic leadership in health IT transformation
- **Chief Information Officer**: Executive leadership in healthcare technology
- **Healthcare Consultant**: Independent consulting in digital health transformation
