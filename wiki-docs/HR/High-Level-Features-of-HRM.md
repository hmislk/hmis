# High-Level Features of Human Resource Management (HRM) Module

## Overview

The Human Resource Management (HRM) module provides comprehensive workforce management capabilities designed specifically for healthcare institutions. This module integrates with hospital operations to manage the complete employee lifecycle from recruitment to retirement, ensuring compliance with Sri Lankan labor regulations while following international hospital HR best practices.

## 5. Leave Management

### 5.1 Leave Types (Sri Lankan Compliance)
**Statutory Leave:**
- **Annual Leave**: 14 days per year (after 12 months of service)
- **Casual Leave**: 7 working days per year
- **Medical/Sick Leave**: 7-14 days (as per company policy)
- **Maternity Leave**:
  - First two confinements: 84 working days
  - Subsequent confinements: 42 working days
- **No-Pay Leave**: Unpaid leave provisions

**Additional Leave Categories:**
- Paternity Leave
- Duty Leave
- Lieu Leave (compensatory)
- Study Leave
- Special Leave

### 5.2 Leave Entitlement Management
- Annual leave entitlement configuration
- Leave accrual rules
- Carry-forward policies
- Pro-rata calculations for new employees
- Entitlement adjustments

### 5.3 Leave Application & Approval
- Leave request submission

- Leave balance validation

- Leave cancellation handling

### 5.4 Leave Processing
